AP Chemistry Prototype

A website made to update the AP Chemistry class of 2020 on homework, resources, and course requirements.

Requirements

Course summary: Students who are accepted into AP Chemistry are expected to possess knowledge of the subject, be willing to put in the work required to maintain a high grade, and be a responsible student.

Items needed:

  • 5 subject notebook
  • Graphing calculator
  • Ruler
  • Writing utensils
  • A brain

Advantages

Here we will cover the material you went over in your regular chemistry class in greater detail and depth, allowing those students participating to gain a greater understanding of chemistry. If you are interested in any type of career in medicine, chemical engineering, or any field of chemistry, this course is essential.
