Chemistry Overview

A website supplying students with an overview of the chemistry course.

Requirements

Course summary: Students who are taking Chemistry are expected to have all the necessary supplies, be ready to take detailed notes, give the teacher their full attention when class begins, and achieve high grades.

Items needed:

  • 5 subject notebook
  • Graphing calculator
  • Ruler
  • Writing utensils (pens, pencils, and highlighters)
  • Graph Paper
  • A brain

Topics

The topics covered in the regents/honors level Chemistry course include, but are not limited to:

  • Math and Lab Skills
  • Physical Behavior of Matter/Energy
  • Atomic Concepts
  • The Periodic Table
  • Nomenclature/Balancing/Reactions
  • Moles/Stoichiometry
  • Chemical Bonding
  • Gases/Gas Laws
  • Solutions/Concentration/Molarity
  • Acids, Bases, and Salts
  • Kinetics and Equilibrium
  • Redox Reactions

Students should possess an understanding of all the above topics and subjects within them by the end of the course.
